GAC Scoring Guide

Part 1: Project & Impact

Description Clarity

Score 5 Highly clear, simple language, no buzzwords. Reads as if explaining to a friend.

Score 1 Unclear, relies heavily on jargon and buzzwords. Inaccessible.

SDG Alignment

Score 5 Identifies a problem related to an SDG and offers a compelling response.

Score 1 No connection to an SDG or offers a poor response.

Part 1: Proximity to Issue

Community Connection

Score 5 Deep, authentic connection. Active member or has impactful relationships with the target group.

Score 1 No connection, biased views, harmful stereotypes, or lacks empathy.

Part 2: Leadership Potential

Personal Motivation

Score 5 Articulates a deep, personal "why." The passion feels lived-in and completely authentic.

Score 1 Unclear motivation. Focused on resume-building or external rewards instead of social commitment.

MCN Values

Score 5 Provides detailed examples of empathetic, humble, and inclusive leadership.

Score 1 Focuses on personal success and contradicts or undermines MCN values.
